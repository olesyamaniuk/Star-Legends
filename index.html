About This Game

Jewels Star 3 is a classic match-3 puzzle game designed to test your strategic thinking, quick decision-making, and pattern recognition skills. The objective is simple - match three or more identical jewels to clear them from the board and complete level goals.

Unlike traditional match-3 games, Jewels Star 3 introduces:

  • Time-Based Challenges: Beat levels within a limited time to earn bonus points.
  • Unique Obstacles: Encounter chained jewels, frozen gems, and solid stones that require special strategies to clear.
  • Powerful Combos: Unlock explosive combinations by matching more than three jewels in a row.

The deeper you go, the harder the puzzles become. Only the most skilled players will master every level and achieve three-star ratings in every challenge.
Start your jewel-matching adventure today!

How to Play

  • Match 3 or More:

    Swap adjacent gems to create a matching set of three or more.

  • Use Power-Ups:

    Utilize Lightning Jewels, Bomb Jewels, and Energy Jewels to clear the board efficiently.

  • Break Obstacles:

    Free frozen jewels, unlock chained gems, and smash stones to complete the level.

  • Reach Level Goals: 

    Each level has specific objectives—clear a number of jewels, beat the timer, or score a certain amount of points.

  • Earn Stars:

    Complete levels efficiently to earn up to three stars.

  • Strategize Moves:

    Limited moves? Think ahead to create combos and maximize your score.

FAQ

  • What is the objective of Jewels Star 3? 
    The goal is to match three or more identical jewels to clear the board and complete level objectives.
  • Can I play offline?
    Yes! Jewels Star 3 is fully playable without an internet connection.
  • How do I earn three stars on a level?
    Finish the level quickly, achieve a high score, and use fewer moves to earn the maximum rating.
  • What happens if I run out of moves?
    You can replay the level or use boosters to help complete your goal.
  • Are there daily rewards? 
    Yes! Log in daily to claim free coins, boosters, and special event prizes.
  • How do I get rid of frozen or chained jewels?
    Match adjacent jewels to break through frozen gems. Chained jewels require multiple matches to unlock.
  • Is the game free to play?
    Yes, but it offers optional in-app purchases for boosters and power-ups.
  • Will there be new levels?
    Yes! Regular updates bring new levels, challenges, and features to the game.
